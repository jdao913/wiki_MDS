In mathematics, Kuramoto–Sivashinsky equation is a fourth-order nonlinear partial differential equation,[1] named after Yoshiki Kuramoto and Gregory Sivashinsky, who derived the equation to model the diffusive instabilities in the laminar flame front in 1970s.
