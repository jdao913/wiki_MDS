Life and Education[edit]
Nizameddin Shirin oghlu Isgenderov was born on 4 March 1956 in Salyan city of Azerbaijan Republic. He studied his secondary school numbered 27 between 1964-1974 in Salyan. He got his mechanics-mathematics degree from BSU[1] university during 1974-1979. Nizameddin Shirin oghlu Isgenderov graduated from BSU in 1979, working as a chief-laboratory between 1979-1981 for ASA in Mechanics-Mathematics Institute, between 1981–1983 years working as an intern-researcher in Ukraine SA in Mathematics Institute, between 1983–1986 years worked as a PhD candidate. He defended his candidacy dissertation under the supervision of L.P.Nijnik in Kiev.[2][3]
EMPLOYMENT HISTORY[edit]
Between 1986-1993 he worked a junior scientist, scientist, and senior scientist in SA in its Mathematics and Mechanics Institute, between 1993–1997 years he worked as a doctorate-professor in Yildiz Technical University of Turkey Republic, between 1997–1999 years he worked as a director of laboratory and as a chief.
Prof. N.S.Isgenderov has been working in BSU since 1999. He has been doctorate of physics-mathematics science since 2003 and currently works as a professor of “Differential and Integral equations” department and dean of Mechanics-Mathematics faculty.[4][5]
He is researching in the field of linear hyperbolic and simple differential equation systems. He has more than 65 published scientific papers, 1 published monographic and 6 PhD students under his supervision. He is teaching Differential equations module for Bachelor’s Degree students and theory of differential operators, transform theory and their applications for master's degree students.
He is head of “Functional Analysis” faculty of Mathematics and Mechanics Institute at ANAS.
He was awarded with “Taraggi” Medal on the basis of Order dated on 30.10.2009 by President of Azerbaijan for his contribution to Azerbaijan education and to the development of science.[6][7]
