Biz Television (commonly referred to as BizTV) is a broadcast television network targeting entrepreneurs and small business owners in the United States. BizTV is the home for your business. Whether you're an entrepreneur, small business owner or just managing your family's assets, BizTV can help. Find the resources you need to manage your money, start your latest venture or grow your business. It’s Biz for You!
Small business generated 64% of all new jobs in the last decade.
There are more than 30 million small businesses and 15 million home-based companies in the United States.  BizTV is the only network committed to original, educational and compelling programming about real people succeeding in business.
BizTV recently became available via streaming video at Ustream. It is the first 24-hour network to broadcast through the Ustream platform.

Programming[edit]
Programs airing on the channel include The Ray Lucia Show, The Big Biz Show, BTV Business TV, Business Rockstars, DW News, Financial Issues with Dan Celia, Green Economy, HomeTalk USA, INN News, Made in Germany, Money TV, Motorz, Radio Night Live with Kevin McCullough, Animal Science, Awesome Adventures, Real Life 101, Talk! with Audrey, Think Big, Walking Wild, Wild Wonders.
The network, based in Arlington, Texas, and airs on digital subchannels of broadcast television stations around the country. You can also join the BizTVClub.com to watch the programming streamed or on demand.
