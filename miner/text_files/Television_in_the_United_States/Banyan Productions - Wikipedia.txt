Banyan Productions is a production company located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States. Clients include Discovery Channel, the Food Network, Fox, HGTV, Lifetime, NBC, Nickelodeon/Nick at Nite, TLC, and the Travel Channel. Banyan Productions was founded in 1992 by Ray Murray, the former host of Evening Magazine on KYW-TV 3 in Philadelphia.
