Bedford Falls Productions (or The Bedford Falls Company) is the production company of Marshall Herskovitz, the co-creator of the television series thirtysomething and Once and Again, and the producer of Legends of the Fall and Blood Diamond. The company is also known for producing the series My So-Called Life, and the Academy Award-winning films Traffic and Shakespeare in Love.
In honor of the film It's a Wonderful Life; the Bedford Falls Company was named after the fictional town, and an overhead view of a facsimile of the Bailey household appears in the production logo, which also features a man and a woman singing the last line of "Buffalo Gals" (a song featured in the movie), "..and dance by the light of the moon."
