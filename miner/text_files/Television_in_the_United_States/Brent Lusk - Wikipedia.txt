Brent Michael Lusk (born October 6, 1982) is an American television editor and visual effects artist. His entertainment career is mostly centered on reality television, sports, commercials & cinema.
Brent Lusk is best known for his work on Little Women: Atlanta, Spaceman (2016 film), Little Women: LA and UFC Ultimate Insider.[1][2]
Biography[edit]
Brent Lusk was born in Green Bay, Wisconsin, Son of Linda and Dean Lusk. He is 1 of 9 children. At the age of 21 he graduated from Full Sail University in Orlando, Florida and moved to Los Angeles, California where he currently works as a television editor and visual effects artist.
