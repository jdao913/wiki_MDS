External links[edit]

Official website

The first and only 24/7 Somali Language Television Station in the U.S, part of WCSN Ohio Multicultural Network, transmitted on digital channel 32.3 & online . Dalmar Television has been the premiere source of Somali related local and international news since 2011, primarily serving the Somali community in Central Ohio and the United States .
