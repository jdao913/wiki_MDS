Dennis the Menace in Mayday for Mother is a 1980 animated TV movie. It was produced by DePatie-Freleng. It is based on Hank Ketcham's 1951 comic strip.
Storyline[edit]
It is Mother's Day and Dennis has not decided what to give his mother for a gift.
