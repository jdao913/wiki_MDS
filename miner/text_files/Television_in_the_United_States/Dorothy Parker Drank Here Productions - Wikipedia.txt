Dorothy Parker Drank Here Productions is the production company created in 1996 by Amy Sherman-Palladino to produce Love and Marriage.[1] The company also produced Gilmore Girls, The Return of Jezebel James,[2] and Bunheads.[3] The name "Dorothy Parker Drank Here" is tribute to writer Dorothy Parker, a major influence on Palladino.
