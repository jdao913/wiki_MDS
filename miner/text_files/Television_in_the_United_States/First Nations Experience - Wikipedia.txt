First Nations Experience (FNX) is a non-profit television network that is owned by San Bernardino Community College District. The network is broadcast from San Bernardino Valley College. The networks’ programming is aimed at Native Americans and Indigenous people.

History[edit]
First Nation Experience was launched on September 25, 2011 through a $6 Million gift from its founding partner San Manuel Band of Mission Indians. On November 1, 2014, FNX became available to hundreds of PBS, community, tribal stations and televisions service providers across the country.[1] In 2015, San Manuel Band of Mission Indians awarded FNX with its second $6 million gift to help expand the station.[2] Since 2016, the channel reaches 36 million household across the country. It was announced in 2017 through its spectrum auction that the station has secured $157 million.
