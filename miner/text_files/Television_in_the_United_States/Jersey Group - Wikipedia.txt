The Jersey Group is the company of Danny DeVito and Rhea Perlman.
Labels[edit]

Jersey Films - The flagship label of the company, a film producer. Jersey Films has produced Man on the Moon, Matilda, Reno 911! Miami, Freedom Writers, How High, Death to Smoochy, Erin Brockovich and many others.
Jersey Records - A record company whose only release was the Man on the Moon soundtrack.
Jersey Television - An independent TV production company that produced Reno 911! and Karen Sisco.
