KSWB-TV Tower is a 68.2 metre high guyed television tower at San Diego, California, United States at 32°41′47″N 116°56′10″W﻿ / ﻿32.69639°N 116.93611°W﻿ / 32.69639; -116.93611Coordinates: 32°41′47″N 116°56′10″W﻿ / ﻿32.69639°N 116.93611°W﻿ / 32.69639; -116.93611. KSWB-TV Tower was built in 1984 and is used by KSWB-TV Channel 69 (Fox 5) and by KNSD Channel 39 (NBC 7) in San Diego.
