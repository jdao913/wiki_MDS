The KWCH Tower is a 1,501 foot high guy-wired aerial mast for the transmission of TV programs and FM in Burrton, Kansas, United States (Geographical coordinates: 38°03′38″N 97°45′50″W﻿ / ﻿38.06056°N 97.76389°W﻿ / 38.06056; -97.76389). The KWCH Tower was completed in 1965 and is the property of KWCH-TV (Schurz Communications doing business as Sunflower Broadcasting).
The tower is the tallest structure in the state of Kansas. Kansas' tallest building is the Epic Center.
