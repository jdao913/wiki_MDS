Telemundo was the first television channel broadcasting telenovelas, to the began in the 1990s, Univision also started producing telenovelas. Over the years these two television networks, have made co-productions with other countries as Venezuela, México, Colombia and Chile. Telemundo and Univision, are channels brothers; Venevisión and Televen, Venezuelan channels that broadcast all their telenovelas for years, and with those who have produced several telenovelas. Telemundo also has as a brother channel to Caracol Televisión, company that also has worked with.
