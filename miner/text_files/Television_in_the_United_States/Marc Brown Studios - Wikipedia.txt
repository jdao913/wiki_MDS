Marc Brown Studios is a TV production company. Following the air of Arthur's Perfect Christmas [1], the Christmas special of the hit cartoon series Arthur, Marc Brown Studios was formed by Marc Brown in 2000 nationwide in the US. Marc Brown is the writer of the original Arthur book series that inspired the TV show.
Starting in July 2003, Marc Brown Studios produced Postcards from Buster.[2]
Company[edit]
Marc Brown Studios is the formal creative, corporate and production entity of children's book author and illustrator, Marc Brown. Formed shortly after the launch of the Arthur television series on PBS, Marc Brown Studios was created to manage the rapidly expanding international licensing and publishing program associated with the show. Marc's oldest son, Tolon Brown, is also a member of the company and contributes to management of the licensing program as well as overseeing the production and development of on-screen media.[3][4]
