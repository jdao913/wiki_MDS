The Nebraska Educational Tower Holdrege is a 1,066 ft (324.8 m) high guyed TV mast in Holdrege, Nebraska, USA. It is the transmission site for Nebraska Educational Telecommunications stations KLNE-TV and KLNE-FM, both licensed to Lexington.
The tower was built in 1965, but destroyed on November 27, 2005 after an aircraft collision.[1] The tower was rebuilt the following year.[when?]
