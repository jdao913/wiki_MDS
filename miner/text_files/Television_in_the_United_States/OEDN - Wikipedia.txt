Founded in October, 2007 - the OpenCable Application Platform (OCAP) EBIF Developer Network (OEDN.net) is an online developer network for the promotion of Interactive Television application and service development on digital cable television.
Dedicated to the goal of supporting the emerging and long-term needs of software engineers and product teams building OCAP (tru2way) and EBIF applications to run not only on digital cable television, but also converged applications and services spanning mobile and broadband devices.
OEDN.net is a networked Community of Practice with a membership constituency drawn from cable companies, ITV application vendors, content providers, programming networks, advertisers, academic interactive media researchers and independent consultants.
