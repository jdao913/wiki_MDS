Pasiones is a Spanish-language cable and satellite television channel owned by Hemisphere Media Group which was launched in 2008. The channel airs telenovelas and series 24 hours a day.[1]
Operating channels[edit]

Pasiones USA - available in the mainland United States and Puerto Rico.
Pasiones Latin America - available in Argentina, Bolivia, Central America, Chile, Colombia, Dominican Republic, Ecuador, Mexico, Paraguay, Peru, Uruguay and Venezuela.
