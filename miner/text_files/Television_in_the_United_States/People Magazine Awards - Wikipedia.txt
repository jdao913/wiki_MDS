The People Magazine Awards is an American awards ceremony that aired on December 18, 2014 at 9 PM to 11 PM. The only ceremony was hosted by Nick Cannon.[1] The ceremony was held at the Beverly Hilton Hotel in Beverly Hills, California.
Performers for the event included: 5 Seconds of Summer and Maroon 5. Jennifer Aniston, Kevin Hart, Kate Hudson, Michael Keaton and Jennifer Lopez made a special appearance during the show. The ceremony was aired on NBC and was produced by Dick Clark Productions with Allen Shapiro, Mike Mahan and Barry Edelman. Carol Donovan and Michael Dempsey were also executive producers for the event.
The People Magazine Awards allowed fans to vote via Twitter using the hashtag #PEOPLEMagazineAwards for the award of Best PEOPLE Magazine Cover of the Year.

Ratings and Reception[edit]
