Reality Binge, is a weekly Fox Reality Channel original series which features reality TV clips & reality commentary every week. The show is hosted by Eric Toms.[1]

History[edit]
Reality Binge premiered on July 10, 2008 on Fox Reality Channel.[2]
It aired its last episode on December 18, 2008.
Format[edit]
Reality Binge features the host, Eric Toms, on a green screen set. The show incorporates various segments that focus on reality television shows on all television networks, both broadcast and cable.
