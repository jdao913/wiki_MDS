ZGS Communications, also referred to as the ZGS Group or ZGS Broadcasting, is a television and radio station operator in the United States, based in Arlington, Virginia. The company operates 13 stations: 11 television stations (with the exception of WZGS-CD, all of which are Telemundo affiliates), and two radio stations. All of their television stations and one radio station broadcast in Spanish (The exception, WILC in Laurel, Maryland, flipped to an English-language conservative talk format on February 3, 2014).
