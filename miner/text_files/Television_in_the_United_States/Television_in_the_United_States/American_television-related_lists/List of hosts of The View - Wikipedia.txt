These are the current and former hosts of The View.
Current Panel[edit]
The original panel consisted of Meredith Vieira, Star Jones, Debbie Matenopoulos and Barbara Walters, whilst Walters shared the fourth seat with Joy Behar for a while, until all five members of the panel appeared together. In 1999 Mantopoulos left the panel and was replaced by Lisa Ling.
