The Pride of America Awards will be an annual event in the United States of America,[1] honouring ordinary people who have acted bravely or extraordinarily in challenging situations.
The awards will mimic those of the Pride of Britain Awards launched in 1999.
The show will be produced by Simon Cowell's television production company Syco.
