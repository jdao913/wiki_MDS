Vibrant TV Network is a multi-platform network that brings previously unseen international programming to mainstream US television audiences. Vibrant TV provides a curated mix of globally produced programming delivered through select cable services, a 24/7 ad-supported live stream, and a subscription based service video-on-demand (SVOD) . Much of the Network's programming is award-winning. The network is headquartered in the Chicago, Illinois area.
Founded by Dan Zifkin of Zephyr Media Group and officially launched in late 2014, the network boasts the motto Turn on the World™. Vibrant TV's programming focuses on diversity of content, offering drama, comedy, lifestyle, sports and movies.

Accessibility[edit]
The Vibrant TV VOD package[1] can be accessed on desktop and mobile devices through its subscription service. Vibrant TV Network is also available on Xumo,[2] Sling TV, Amazon Fire TV, Roku, FilmOn, YIP-TV, Opera TV and on Panasonic and Sony Smart TV's. The service allows viewers to watch available episodes of Vibrant TV's stable of content. The Network is also available on select cable services.[citation needed]
