The TV Alabama tower is a 2,000-foot (610 m) tall guyed television mast located near Windham Springs, in Tuscaloosa County, Alabama, United States. TV Alabama Tower was built in 1996 and is the property of "TV Alabama, Inc."
The TV Alabama tower is one of two masts broadcasting the feed from WBMA-LD, serving Anniston/Gadsden, Birmingham, and Tuscaloosa.
