Doozer is the production company of Bill Lawrence,[2] best known for making Scrubs. The company's name is taken from a variant of Lawrence's middle name, Van Duzer. They currently are under contract with Warner Bros. Television. Jeff Ingold, former head of comedy at NBC, serves as president.[3] Randall Winston is the final head of the triumvirate, acting as a main producer on all Doozer series.
