Little Stranger, Inc. is a film and television production company founded by actress and producer Tina Fey with executive vice president and long-time collaborator Eric Gurian.[1] It is known for producing the long-running series 30 Rock and Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt.

In February 2016, Little Stranger signed with Universal Pictures on a two-year first look production deal.[1]
