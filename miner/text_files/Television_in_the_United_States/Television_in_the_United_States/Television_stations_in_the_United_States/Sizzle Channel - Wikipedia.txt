Sizzle Channel was founded in 2013 and is a digital cable television network in the United States.
Sizzle features programming targeting 18- to 45-year-olds, with programming blocks targeted more to males or females depending on the daypart. Sizzle's programming is different then other cable networks in that most programs are between 7â€“14 minutes in length just like most web series and shows found on the internet. The unique programming format allows viewers to see an average of 6.5 different shows per hour on a variety of diverse topics and themes.
Programming[edit]
The bulk of Sizzle's programming comes from content partners that are established media brands.
Distribution[edit]
Sizzle Channel is available in around 30 million cable households in the United States and Puerto Rico. The channel can also be viewed live on the internet via their home page.
