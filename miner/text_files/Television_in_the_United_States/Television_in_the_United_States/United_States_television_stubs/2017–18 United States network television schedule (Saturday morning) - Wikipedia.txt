This was the United States broadcast television schedule on the four commercial television networks airing entertainment programming on Saturday mornings for the season beginning in the fall of 2017 and ending in the spring of 2018.
On Fox, Xploration Station and its predecessor, the infomercial block Weekend Marketplace, remains available, and Fox affiliates can carry either lineup.
On The CW, One Magnificent Morning will reduced to three hours begining this season, the 7:00 a.m.–8:00 a.m. hour was returned back to the affiliates as a result.
