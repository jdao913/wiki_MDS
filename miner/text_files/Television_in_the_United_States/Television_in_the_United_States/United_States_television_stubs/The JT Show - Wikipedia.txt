The JT Show (formerly known as The JT & Dave Show and Air Bubba) is an American talk show on Supertalk Mississippi. It is hosted by JT Williamson and produced by Ryne "Ryno" Montgomery. It premiered in August 2010. It has since featured the voices of several local, Mississippi and national politicians.
Music[edit]
The theme song for the show is Livin' on the Edge by Aerosmith and the closing song is Courtesy of the Red, White, and Blue by Toby Keith. The show also features several heavy metal, rock and country songs, and even a little bluegrass.
