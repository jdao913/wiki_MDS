Daniel Dae Kim


3AD is a film and television production company established by actor/producer Daniel Dae Kim as part of a two-year first look development deal with CBS Studios.[1][2]
As of May 2014, the company is in talks to remake the popular Korean medical drama Good Doctor for the network.[3] The company's current personnel includes Head of Development Lindsay Goffman, Development Executive Keo Lee and Executive Assistant Mark Rozeman.
