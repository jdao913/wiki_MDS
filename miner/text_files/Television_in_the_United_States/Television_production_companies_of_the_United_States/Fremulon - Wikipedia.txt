Fremulon is a television production company founded by television producer and writer Michael Schur.[1] It is known for producing the long-running series Parks and Recreation and Brooklyn Nine-Nine. The name "Fremulon" comes from the fake insurance company that Michael Schur's pseudonym, Ken Tremendous, worked at when Schur was writing for the blog Fire Joe Morgan.[2]
