History[edit]
Started in 1995, Hip Entertainment Group sought to help bridge the gap between a Spanish and English TV audience.
Networks[edit]
Hip TV Inc., a division of Hip Entertainment Group, is responsible for over 1000 hours of original programming. Their programs have appeared on such networks as:

Travel Channel
Spike
USA Network
Nickelodeon
Discovery Kids
