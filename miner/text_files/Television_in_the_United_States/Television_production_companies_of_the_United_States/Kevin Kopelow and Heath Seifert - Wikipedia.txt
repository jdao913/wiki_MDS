Kevin Kopelow (born 1963[1] in Los Angeles, California) and Heath Seifert (born 1968[1] in Los Angeles, California) are an American television writing and producing team. They have written and produced for All That, Kenan & Kel, Austin & Ally (also creators of the latter), among other series.[2]
As an actor, Kopelow is best known for appearing as Kevin, the stage manager on All That, as well as appearing as a panelist on the game show Figure It Out.
