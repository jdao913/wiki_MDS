The Curiosity Company, Inc. is an American production company behind hit series Futurama and the 1999 television film Olive, the Other Reindeer. The company logo depicts the letter "C" being formed by a surfboard reflection against various ocean waves, alongside distorted water sounds. The footage and sounds are taken from A Study in Wet, a short film made by Matt's father, Homer.[1]
