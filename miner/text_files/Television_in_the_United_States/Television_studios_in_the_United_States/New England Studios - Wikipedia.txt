New England Studios is a movie studio located in Devens, Massachusetts.[1]
New England Studios officially opened on September 16, 2013.[2]
It is only 40 minutes outside of Boston, MA. The studio has 24-hour gated security and parking for 271 cars and trucks.[3]
It consists of (4) 18,000 sq ft sound stages that occupy the majority of the land. NE Studios hosted its first major movie, Tumbledown back in March. The studio is now looking into other projects that it can be the host of. It aspires to have regular TV shows booked into the studios, but is still looking into viable options for the studio.[4]
