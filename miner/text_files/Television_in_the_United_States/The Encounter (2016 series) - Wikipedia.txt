Synopsis[edit]
What if you had a personal encounter with Jesus Christ? Based on the popular movies, the series follows individual life stories and illustrates how an unexpected experience with Jesus Christ can shift lives into a new and purposeful direction.

"Heist"
October 21, 2016 (2016-10-21)



A desperate pair of brothers are attempting a convenience store "heist" that has turned potentially lethal. With time running out, can “The Man", appearing as the store’s cashier, deliver a less deadly outcome to the situation?
Fun Fact #1: The bulletin board that showed the photos of Cal's future can be seen in the background before Cal even discovered it.
Fun Fact #2: On the bulletin board, the photos that show Cal's wife is that of Alexa PenaVega, Carlos PenaVega's real-life wife.
Fun Fact #3: Many of the store products' names in the episode were covered up so the production company would not face a potential lawsuit.
Guest Stars: Hutch Dano as J.D., Carlos PenaVega as Cal Dawson, Dane E. Connor as Perry
