The Outdoor Experience is an outdoor talk show airing on Hunt Channel (Dish) and on Gen7 Outdoors (Roku).[1] The show is hosted by 17-year-old Blake Alma who previously has hosted a radio show.[2] The show premiered on Monday, May 22nd at 10 PM EST and airs weekly on Hunt Channel.[3]
Some notable guests on the show include Nick Mundt from the Bone Collector crew, Reed Robertson, EJ Snyder, among other outdoorsmen.[4]
