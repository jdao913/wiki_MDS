Waterman Broadcasting Corporation is a company based in Fort Myers, Florida. It was founded in 1978 in Ft. Myers when it purchased WBBH.
It was founded by Bernard Waterman [1].
Stations owned by Waterman[edit]

WBBH-TV channel 20 / cable 2 (NBC), Fort Myers, Florida
WZVN-TV channel 26 / cable 7 (ABC), Naples, Florida (owned by Montclair Communications; operated by Waterman on a local marketing agreement)
WVIR-TV channel 29 / cable 4 (NBC/The CW/WeatherNation TV), Charlottesville, Virginia
